About Us

We're Final Year Bachelor Degree Multimedia Computing student from Liverpool John Moores University which currently pursuing to be a professional website developer and animator. Our journey started in 2020 as Degree student in studying various information technology and core multimedia subject. This current development on this website is to encouraged children to learn and explore stuff in the website through watching educational animation cartoon and challenging game puzzle. We believed that through this edutainment website aimed for children under 6 years old, the website application can contribute many positive factors to the children as well as their parents. This ecosystem consists of software, education and community will help the future generation to develop higher thinking skill for a better development growth.
